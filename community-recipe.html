Korean Bulgogi (Fire Meat)

45 mins 4 servings Medium
4.5 (32 ratings)

This authentic Korean beef bulgogi recipe has been handed down from my grandmother. The marinade creates incredibly tender beef with a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors. Serve with steamed rice and banchan (Korean side dishes) for a complete meal.

Ingredients

Servings: 4
  • 1.5 lbs thinly sliced beef (ribeye or sirloin)
  • 1/2 pear, peeled and grated
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp green onions, chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, plus more for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1

    In a large bowl, combine the pear, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, green onions, and sesame seeds to make the marinade.

  2. 2

    Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade and mix well to ensure all the meat is coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for best results.

  3. 3

    Heat a large skillet or grill pan over high heat. Working in batches, cook the marinated beef for 2-3 minutes until browned and slightly caramelized.

  4. 4

    Garnish with additional sesame seeds and sliced green onions. Serve immediately with steamed rice.
